Salalah Family Trip 🌴

10-Day Magical Khareef Adventure 2026

Practical Family Info

💱

Currency & Cash

1 OMR ≈ 9.5 AED. Keep small Omani Rial notes handy for those cute roadside fruit stalls, wadi boat rides, and tipping. Malls and restaurants easily take cards.

🚙

Getting Around

Renting a sturdy SUV is highly recommended! You'll love the freedom. Just remember: mountain roads get super misty and foggy, so keep those headlights on and drive slowly.

🧥

What to Pack

It's cool and drizzly! Bring light rain jackets, colorful umbrellas (great for photos!), slip-resistant shoes for wet grass, and the best mosquito repellent you can find for wadi days.

Your Daily Adventure

Day 1: 19 July – Arrival & City Magic ✨

  • 🏨
    Settle In & Relax Check into Al Salama Suites. After unpacking, take a gentle afternoon stroll to see the stunning exterior architecture of the Sultan Qaboos Mosque.
  • 🛝
    Kids Playtime Head to Salalah Park. Grab a hot, sweet Karak tea while the kids burn off airplane energy on the swings and green family walkways.
  • 🛍️
    Evening Souq Stroll Walk through Al Haffa Souq as the sun sets. The air smells amazingly like rich frankincense! Look for silver jewelry, colorful Omani textiles, and buy the kids some sweet local Halwa.
  • 🍛
    Dinner Feast on flavorful Pakistani/Indian grills at Al Ghad Restaurant (~AED 28-57). Finish up with a fancy hot cocoa or latte at The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf.

Day 2: 20 July – Emerald Wadis & Boats 🚣‍♀️

  • 🌿
    Wadi Darbat Drive east into lush, emerald green valleys. The waterfalls here are active during Khareef, creating a misty, fairytale atmosphere.
  • 🚤
    Kids Highlight: Boat Ride! Rent a cute pedal boat or a small motorboat (~AED 28-48) to cruise the turquoise lake. Keep your eyes peeled for wild camels grazing right by the water!
  • 🧺
    Springs & Castles Bring a picnic mat to Ayn Razat springs for a shaded rest. On the way back, stop by the historical Taqah Castle for a quick history lesson.
  • 🥥
    Food & Snacks Stop at roadside stalls for fresh, incredibly cheap local mangoes and coconuts. For dinner, experience sitting on traditional floor cushions in a private family room at Bin Ateeq (~AED 38-67).

Day 3: 21 July – Wild Coast & Blowholes 🌊

  • 🏖️
    Al Mughsail Beach Head west along a spectacular coastal road with hairpin bends. You'll arrive at a dramatic coastline featuring miles of soft white sand.
  • 💦
    The Roaring Blowholes Walk up to Marneef Cave's shaded viewing decks. The kids will scream with joy when the ocean waves crash into the rocks, shooting water up to 30 meters high through natural vents! (Hold little hands tightly).
  • 🐚
    Sand Play Spend downtime walking the massive beach collecting unique shells, though swimming isn't advised due to strong Khareef currents.
  • 🍤
    Cliffside Dining Eat lunch at Al Marnife Restaurant right on the cliffs (~AED 48-95), offering great seafood and Arabic dishes with an unbeatable view of the wild ocean.

Day 4: 22 July – History & Cool Malls 🏛️

  • 🏺
    Museum Morning Visit the Al Baleed Archaeological Site & Museum of the Frankincense Land (~AED 19-28). It’s super educational and a great way to escape the morning humidity.
  • 🕹️
    Mall Retreat Head to Salalah Gardens Mall. It’s huge, perfectly air-conditioned, and has an awesome indoor arcade/play zone with token rides that the kids will absolutely love.
  • 🍕
    Kid-Approved Food Grab a super easy, highly-rated lunch at Makdas Fried Chicken & Pizza (~AED 19-48). Parents can grab another great coffee at The Coffee Bean inside the mall.
  • 🛒
    Shopping Spree Use this time to stock up on any forgotten clothes, buy toys for the hotel room, and grab familiar groceries or snacks from the large supermarket.

Day 5: 23 July – Above the Clouds ☁️

  • ⛰️
    Jabal Samhan Drive high up the winding mountain roads. You will literally be standing *above* the thick white clouds! It’s chilly up here, so zip up those jackets.
  • 💧
    Ayn Athum Falls Descend into the misty forests to see the curtain-like cascade of Ayn Athum Waterfall. Keep an eye out for the massive, thick-trunked Baobab trees along the route!
  • 🐫
    Nature Rides Look for locals offering safe, guided short horseback or camel rides for the kids around the green plains (~AED 48-95).
  • 🍽️
    Fancy Feast Treat the family to a premium dining experience at the Sakalan Buffet at the gorgeous Anantara Resort (~AED 95-143+). Great international options for picky eaters!

Day 6: 24 July – Festival Lights & Beach 🎡

  • ⛱️
    Lazy Morning Take it easy today. Have a slow breakfast, let the kids splash in the hotel pool, or build sandcastles on the calmer stretches of Al Haffa Beach.
  • 🎪
    Khareef Festival As evening falls, head to the bustling Khareef Festival Grounds! It’s brightly lit and filled with traditional Omani music, dance performances, candy floss, and fun fairground rides for the kids.
  • 🍝
    Seaside Dinner Dine at the Ocean Blue Beach House (~AED 76-114). It’s a beautiful Mediterranean spot where the kids have plenty of open space to play safely while you eat.
  • ☕
    Sweet Treats End the night with famous fluffy, buttery coffee buns at Paparoti or Hurof Café.

Day 7 & 8: 25-26 July – Frankincense & Mountains 🌿

  • 🌳
    Day 7: The Frankincense Trail Take a scenic, quiet drive out west to spot authentic wild frankincense trees growing on the rocky terrain. Enjoy a hearty, cheap meal at Al Qadri (~AED 28-57).
  • 🕌
    Day 8: Sacred Views Drive through deep mist to Prophet Job’s Tomb on the mountaintop. The serenity and views are unmatched.
  • 🎁
    Final Souq Run Head back to Al Haffa for last-minute gifts. *Pro tip:* Ask vendors to double-bag your frankincense resin so it doesn't make all your clothes smell in the suitcase! Eat traditional food tonight at Haffa House (~AED 38-76).

Day 9 & 10: 27-28 July – Favorites & Farewells 👋

  • 🔄
    Day 9: Repeat the Magic Keep this day completely free! Ask the kids what their absolute favorite thing was (Wadi Darbat boat ride? The mall? The blowholes?) and go do it one more time!
  • ✈️
    Day 10: Goodbye Salalah Enjoy a final relaxing hotel breakfast. Do a quick sweep of the room, check out of Al Salama Suites, and grab any last-minute treats at the airport duty-free before flying home.

The Best Family Travel Hack!

When the kids get restless or cranky in the back of the rental car, pull over at one of the bright fruit stands lining the tropical roads. For less than 1 OMR (~9 AED), the friendly vendors will expertly slice the top off a cold, fresh local coconut (Mashmoom) and pop a straw in it. It’s healthy, refreshing, super fun for kids to drink from, and stops the crying instantly!
